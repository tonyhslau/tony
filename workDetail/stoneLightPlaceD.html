-

這是木偶的內在和外在思維的戰鬥，比喻著兩個天使。
這兩位天使外表相同但卻留在不同的地方：洞穴和蒲公英花園。
在洞穴裡，天使一直在為蒲公英而奮鬥，但在她醒來後卻沒有成功。
在蒲公英花園，天使非常高興，並在她醒來後與木偶玩耍。
在洞穴中，天使希望木偶醒來。
但是在蒲公英花園裡，天使希望木偶繼續睡覺。
最後，兩位天使都變成了蒲公英。
木偶醒了。

This is the battle of the inner and outer of the puppet's mind, which implied two angels.

The two angels who are the same appearance but stay at a different place: Cave and Garden of Dandelion.

At the cave, the angel keeps strive for the dandelion chr but unsuccessful after she wakes up.

At the Garden of Dandelion, the angel is very happy and play with the puppet after she wakes up.

In the cave, angel hopes the puppet wake up. But in the Garden of Dandelion, angel hopes the puppet keep sleeping.

Finally, both angels are transformed to the dandelion.

And the puppet is wake up.
