有一群人，每天都會到神門前排隊求水，而只有「完美」的人才能穿過神門得到聖水。阿宇是半人半魚的物種，他來到神門前，侍衛會讓他進入嗎？在這個不允許「不完美特質」下，阿宇該何去何從？

In the middle of an empty desert, people are struggling to survive. Only the 'perfect' human are allowed to get water from the guards. Yu, an odd creature with half human and half fish, came upon the gate for water, what will happen?
