Spider Table

2018
  • Credits:
  • Design
    :     Lau Ho Shing

The furniture design is based on Japanese low table.

It is in spider shape that aim of making this chair is to reduce peoples’ fear of spider.

The shape of spider also makes the chair looks flexible and viscoelastic,

which means the chair will be pressed down if it bears heavy things.

It can make the user be more comfortable when he or she sits on it.



The Spider has segmented bodies with jointed limbs and eight legs.

They use eight legs to sustain their body.
